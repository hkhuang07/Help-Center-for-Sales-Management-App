Login Screen User Guide

Welcome to the user guide for the **Login Screen** of the **HK.Huang Sales Management Software**. This screen is your gateway to accessing the system and ensures that only authorized personnel can manage business operations. This guide will walk you through the components of the login interface and how to use it effectively.

How to Log In

To access the HK.Huang Sales Management Software, follow these steps:

1. Enter Your Credentials

  • Username Field: Enter your registered username into the "Username" text box.
  • Password Field: Enter your corresponding password into the "Password" text box.

Tip: After entering your username, you can press the Enter key to automatically move the cursor to the password field, as handled by txtUserName_KeyDown method.

2. Submit Your Credentials (btnLogin_Click)

Once you have entered both your username and password, you have two ways to proceed:

  • Click Login Button: Click the Login button.
  • Press Enter Key: If your cursor is in the password field, you can press the Enter key to submit your credentials, which triggers the btnLogin_Click method.

The system will then verify your credentials against its database. If successful, you will be directed to the Main Interface; otherwise, an error message will be displayed (e.g., "Incorrect login name!" or "Incorrect password!").

Cancel Login / Exit Application (btnCancel_Click)

If you decide not to log in, or wish to exit the application directly from the login screen, you can:

  • Click Cancel Button: Click the Cancel button. This action will close the login window, allowing you to exit the application.
Security Note:
Your password is encrypted using a secure hashing algorithm (like BCrypt) to protect your account. Always keep your login credentials confidential and avoid sharing them. If you forget your password, please contact your system administrator.
